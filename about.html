About Us

Hare Krishna Bath Fittings LLP.

Hare Krishna Bath Fittings LLP is a young & dynamic company based out at Surat, Gujarat operating in the area of CP Fittings and Bathroom Accessories, offering a big variety of designerproducts range under the brand name Kindle for every class of customer.

We are the company which gives bathroom solutions to every need of any industry or individual and fit into every pocket’s budget. The entire range of product is available PAN India in all major cities through our distributor network.

Our Infrastructure

Over a period of last five years we have invested mainly in developing the basic infrastructure for the benefit of the business. Infrastructural development like locating to bigger premises, increased production & storage capacity, better transportation for people as well as goods etc. enable us to offer extremely competitive yet high quality products to our customers. This investment pays us in processing the goods in a faster and convenient way with more accuracy, increasing the efficiency of the man power and cost effectiveness of the company as a whole.
